My completed projects, in no specific order

Research on TBI and Music Therapy

My thesis for college was on traumatic brain injury and how music therapy can positively impact physical and mental therapy for patients experiencing a traumatic brain injury.

Essentially, I found that music therpy is in fact helpful in the healing process, but it should not replace any other therapy, only included in the standard care.

Research on Eduction and Vaccines

In high school I conducted a research project on the efficacy of education on vaccination rates within the homeless population.

In this research I found that those who were given education were more likely to get ther flu vaccine because they were able to understand what was fact and what was a myth.
